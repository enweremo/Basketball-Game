To activate the start button, the player must enter a username with a minimum of six characters. The objective of the game is to catch as many basketballs as possible by moving the basket net left or right using the "<" or ">" buttons, while the balls randomly fall from the top. The player has 2 minutes until the timer runs out, and the score increases with each ball caught. The total score is displayed at the end of the game, and the player can start a new game by clicking the "Start" button.
